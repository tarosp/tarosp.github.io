About

I am a PhD student at the University of Copenhagen in the department of mathematics. More specifically, I am working at the centre for the Mathematics of Quantum Theory (QMATH) under the supervision of Prof. Laura Mančinska.

Prior to that, I was supported by a scholarship of the Hasler foundation to conduct research in quantum information theory under the supervision of Prof. Anand Natarajan at MIT.

I have completed a master's in computational science and engineering at Harvard University and a master's in mathematical and theoretical physics at the university of Oxford. I have received my bachelor's degree in physics from ETH Zürich.

During the pandemic, I have conducted research in theoretical high energy physics. More precisely, I have worked in the field of the conformal bootstrap in collaboration with Prof. Joao Penedones and Dr. Marco Meineri at EPFL.

You can read more about some of the topics I have studied and the research I have completed during my studies in the Research section of this website.

In parallel to my scientific interests, I have always developed some of my artistic interests, including filmmaking and digital art. You can read more about what I have done in these areas in the Art section of this website.
